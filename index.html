About

I'm a Senior Data Scientist at Fidelity's AI Center of Excellence, where I work across the full LLM stack — from pre-training transformer models on large-scale enterprise data, to retrieval and RAG systems over unstructured text, to safety and guardrails for deployed models.

My work spans session modeling and intent prediction on clickstream data, conversation and transcript analytics with retrieval-augmented summarization, enterprise LLM safety and red-teaming, and building assistive copilots for internal workflows. I care a lot about making these systems reliable enough to actually deploy.

Before Fidelity, I was at ZS Associates working on pharma analytics, explainable AI for ML predictions, and mixed-integer optimization for compliance-heavy program design.

I did my MS by Research at IIT Madras with the ADOPT group (advisor: Prof. P. Ramu), working on interpretable Self-Organizing Maps for design-space exploration and optimization — published in Springer's Structural and Multidisciplinary Optimization and presented at WCSMO-13 in Beijing. I've continued collaborating with IIT Madras since, including a 2023 Heliyon paper applying data-driven methods to social capital in Farmer Producer Companies. Peer-reviewed work is listed on Google Scholar.

I'm drawn to problems where research meets production: pre-training, retrieval, alignment, and evaluation. I like small, composable systems. I like shipping.

Projects

Tools and open-source I've built.

  • CoexistAI Open source · ★ 470

    LLM-driven research automation framework. FastAPI + LangChain, multimodal inputs (web, Reddit, YouTube, PDFs, code), SearxNG retrieval, and pluggable LLMs (Gemini, OpenAI, Ollama) with async APIs and geospatial reasoning.

  • smallGPT From scratch · LLMs

    From-scratch implementations of core LLM internals: GPT-2, Flash Attention, Rotary embeddings, Multi-Head Latent Attention, speculative decoding, online softmax, and scalar autodiff — a playground for understanding modern transformer stacks end to end.

  • parameter-golf-experimentations OpenAI Parameter Golf · 8×H100

    119+ structured experiments for the OpenAI Parameter Golf competition — training the smallest LM that fits in 16 MB in 10 minutes on 8×H100. Covers baseline ablations, Muon optimizer, parallel residuals, meta-TTT, and architectural rewrites. Best result so far: 1.0744 bpb on legal_ttt.

  • bpb.wtf ML experimentation viz

    Interactive mind-map for visualizing ML experimentation journeys — what you tried, what worked, what didn't, and why. Concept-level nodes with organic Bezier connections, sentiment indicators, and an optional LLM-powered Python API. Built for Parameter Golf but reusable for any experiment workflow.
